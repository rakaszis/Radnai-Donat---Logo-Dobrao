Dobrão para Berimbau



Is Capoeira your passion? Do you love playing on your berimbau? You can order your dobrão from Hungary. A great accessory for any berimbau aficionado. These unique metal cast dobrãos antique silver color feature front etchings of a berimbau and famed Mestre Bimba, Mestre Pastinha or your logo. Each dobrão is 4,2cm in diameter and 5 mm thick.

It is used to diversify the sound of the Berimbau.When attached to the metal string it produces low sound and vice versa when not attached to the metal string the sound becomes higher.The dobrão is the heart of the berimbau.Shipping is 4-5 business days within Europe, and 5-10 business days worldwide. The minimum order quantity is 10.
